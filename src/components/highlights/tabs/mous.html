MoU between Silicon Craft VLSI and Prestige Institute of Management & Research, Bhopal

Apr 2024

Silicic Innova Technologies Pvt Ltd, along with Silicon Craft VLSI, has signed an MoU with Prestige Institute of Management & Research, Bhopal. Focus Areas: - Upskilling students in VLSI & Embedded Systems - Faculty development programs - R&D collaboration to enhance semiconductor innovation
