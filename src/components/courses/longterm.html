Long Term Courses

Advanced ASIC Design & Verification

Master ASIC front-end design and verification with SystemVerilog, UVM, and industry-standard flows. You’ll learn to design, verify, and validate digital systems from RTL to sign-off using real projects and professional tools.

Physical Design

Gain expertise in the back-end design flow — from netlist to GDSII. This course focuses on timing, power, and area optimization using real EDA tools, preparing you for a Physical Design Engineer role in top semiconductor companies.

FPGA-Based System Design

Learn to design, implement, and test complete digital systems on FPGAs. This course bridges theory and hardware by teaching you how to bring RTL designs to life on real boards with on-board debugging.

Advanced Embedded Systems

Become a hands-on embedded engineer through real-time projects on microcontrollers, RTOS, and Linux-based IoT systems. You’ll learn to build firmware from scratch and interface hardware using modern embedded workflows.
